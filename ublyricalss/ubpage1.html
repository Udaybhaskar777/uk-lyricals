AAWARA

Shiva is a carefree young man living in Bangalore, searching for a job with his loyal group of friends. One day, he spots a beautiful woman named Charulatha ("Charu"), who is also job hunting. Instantly attracted to her, he frequently follows her.

While waiting at a railway station for a friend, Shiva meets a tense Charu and a Telugu-speaking man. Mistaking Shiva for a cab driver, they request a ride to Chennai. Excited, Shiva accepts the offer, leading to an unexpected journey.
